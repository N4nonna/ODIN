Singapore Shrimp Curry

Ingredients

  • 4 firm bananas
  • Melted butter or margarine
  • Salt
  • 1 pound fresh shrimp, cleaned and cooked
  • 2 cups hot cooked rice
  • Curry Sauce, at right

Instructions

  1. Set oven for moderately hot, 375Â°, and grease a shallow baking dish.
  2. Peel bananas; cut in half, crosswise, and arrange in the dish.
  3. Brush with melted butter; sprinkle lightly with salt.
  4. Bake 15 to 18 minutes or until bananas are fork tender.
  5. Meanwhile, place shrimp in the top of a double boiler; heat over hot water.
  6. When hot, place shrimp on a bed of cooked rice and arrange cooked bananas around edge.
  7. Pour part of the Curry Sauce on top; serve remainder on the side.
  8. Makes 4 servings.
